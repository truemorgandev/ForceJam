FORCEJAM

Tactical Multitool for the Ukrainian Armed Forces

About

FORCEJAM is a compact and affordable tactical device designed to enhance the safety and communication efficiency of Ukrainian frontline units. It detects enemy FPV drones by scanning key radio frequencies and can jam hostile signals to protect soldiers on the ground.

Additionally, FORCEJAM enables encrypted radio communication between fighters without relying on cellular networks or the internet, ensuring secure and reliable messaging under battlefield conditions.

Our mission is to provide accessible electronic warfare tools that save lives and improve operational coordination in the field.

Features

  • Real-time monitoring of radio frequencies: 433MHz, 868MHz, and 2.4GHz to detect FPV drone signals.
  • Manual activation of drone jamming on selected frequencies using powerful RF modules with optional signal amplifiers for extended range.
  • Secure encrypted messaging with AES-256 protocol, allowing fighters to communicate safely over radio channels without internet.
  • User-friendly touchscreen interface with on-screen keyboard for message input.
  • Two-factor authentication via NFC cards to secure device access and message encryption/decryption.
  • Modular hardware design with plans for future GPS integration and 5.8GHz frequency support.

Hardware

The core of FORCEJAM is built on the versatile ESP32 microcontroller, chosen for its processing power and integrated wireless capabilities.

Communication modules include three nRF24L01+ PA/LNA units and two CC1101 transceivers covering 433MHz and 868MHz bands. The device features a 4-inch color touchscreen for easy operation.

Power is supplied by a rechargeable Li-Po battery, ensuring portability and reliability in the field. Additional signal amplifiers can be connected to increase jamming range.
