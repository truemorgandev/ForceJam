FORCEJAM

Tactical Multitool for the Ukrainian Armed Forces.

FORCEJAM is a compact, modular device designed to detect, jam FPV drones, and enable secure encrypted communication on the battlefield. Our mission is to empower frontline soldiers with affordable and reliable electronic warfare tools to save lives and improve coordination.

About FORCEJAM

In modern warfare, drone surveillance and attacks present a serious threat to ground forces. FORCEJAM was developed to counter this threat by actively monitoring key radio frequencies used by FPV drones, alerting soldiers to incoming reconnaissance, and disrupting hostile drones through targeted jamming.

Furthermore, the device offers encrypted messaging capabilities using AES-256, allowing frontline units to communicate securely over radio frequencies without relying on unstable or compromised cellular networks.

This open and modular project aims to be accessible, cost-effective, and adaptable to evolving battlefield needs.

Features

  • Multi-band RF monitoring: Real-time scanning of 433MHz, 868MHz, and 2.4GHz bands to detect FPV drone signals.
  • Drone jamming: Manual activation of jamming signals on specific frequencies using high-performance RF modules with optional signal amplifiers for extended range.
  • Encrypted messaging: AES-256 based secure communication allowing message transmission over radio channels offline, bypassing internet and cellular networks.
  • User interface: Responsive 4-inch color touchscreen with on-screen keyboard for ease of use in the field.
  • Access control: Two-factor authentication using NFC cards for device access and message encryption/decryption.
  • Modular hardware: Designed for future expansions, including GPS integration and 5.8GHz frequency support.

Hardware Specifications

The core processing is handled by an ESP32 microcontroller, providing the balance of power efficiency and wireless capabilities needed for this mission-critical device.

Communication is facilitated by three nRF24L01+ PA/LNA modules and two CC1101 transceivers, covering the main frequency bands of interest (433MHz, 868MHz, and 2.4GHz).

The user interacts with the device through a 4-inch full-color touchscreen, designed for outdoor visibility and responsive control.

Power is supplied by a rechargeable Li-Po battery, ensuring portability and reliable operation in field conditions. Additional signal amplifiers can be connected to extend jamming range.
